About STAR'S TECH

STAR'S TECH delivers a fully eco-friendly solution through its proprietary upcycling technology.

1

Starfish (Marine Waste) Upcycling

Reuses discarded starfish as de-icers and greatly reduces the cost of incinerating marine waste.

2

Cosmetic ingredients and fertilizer 100% Upcycling

With Penellagen®, a collagen ingredient developed from starfish extracts, and the 'FERTISTAR' liquid fertilizer, we achieve perfect upcycling with zero by-products.

World's only carbon credit certified solution

A starfish-derived porous structure adsorbs chloride ions that cause environmental pollution, creating carbon reduction and marine purification effects.
