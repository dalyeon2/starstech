Key Technology

Using STARSTECH technology to extract a porous structure from starfish ossicles, we ensure performance and reliability.

Key Technology 01

Upcycling marine waste (starfish)

The porous structure from marine-waste starfish adsorbs chloride ions physically and chemically, cutting de-icer pollution at the source.

Key Technology 02

Proprietary controlled-release system

Corrosion inhibitors are stored inside the porous structure and released gradually, maximizing protection with minimal additives.

Key Technology 03

High-performance bead molding and special coating

Special-coated beads speed melting and prevent caking during storage. They also suppress fine dust to reduce inhalation and pet paw irritation.
