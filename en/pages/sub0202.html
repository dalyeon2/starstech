Key Features

ECO-ST Product Features

01

Safe surface protection even at -35C

Prevents damage or discoloration to wood, stone, and composite finishes even in severe cold, and suppresses spalling and surface cracking on winter roads, extending the lifespan of facilities.

02

Excellent compatibility with brick and steel

Coating technology prevents salt damage to protect brick and natural stone, and minimizes corrosion on steel ramps and stairs to keep surfaces stable through winter.

03

Minimizes plant yellowing from chloride ions

Starfish-derived components greatly reduce chloride ions harmful to growth, minimizing damage while keeping landscapes and flowerbeds safe, with reliable de-icing performance.

04

Safe de-icer for pets and people

Bead-shaped design and special coating prevent paw burns, suppress dust, and allow safe use for both people and animals without respiratory irritation.
