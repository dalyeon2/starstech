Founding Story

STARSTECH began by developing a highly efficient, eco-friendly de-icer from starfish.

The idea came when our CEO, Seung-chan Yang, was a corporal. During a military start-up contest, he proposed using starfish, which harm marine ecosystems, to create a de-icer.

Traditional calcium chloride de-icers corrode roads and vehicles, and pollute soil. STARSTECH solved this by creating a non-corrosive, eco-friendly product from the porous structure of starfish.

Recognized for its business and environmental value, the company was founded in Nov 2017. In partnership with government agencies, we upcycle incinerated starfish into de-icers, cosmetics, and fertilizers.

Vision for Tomorrow

Global Sustainable Essential Technologies & Materials Provider

STARSTECH has proven sustainable value through upcycling technology using starfish. Under the goal of "Global Sustainable Essential Technologies & Materials Provider", we aim to go beyond starfish and transform diverse industrial waste and unused resources into high-value essential materials.

By expanding our business with circular resources such as waste acid from semiconductors, we will become a global climate-tech company that sustainably supplies the technologies and materials industries need in the era of the climate crisis.
