Sustainability

Certified carbon credits cumulative 56,000+ tons A sustainable future built by STARSTECH

By replacing the incineration process caused by starfish disposal, we were certified for carbon credits of 26,081 tCO2-eq* in 2023 and 30,087 tCO2-eq in 2024.

Over two years, we have accumulated more than 56,000 tons of carbon credits, building a model where the more you use our products, the more you reduce carbon. The moment you choose our products becomes a carbon reduction action, a virtuous cycle and a sustainable future built by STARSTECH.

*tCO2-eq: CO2 equivalent per ton of greenhouse gases

Impact

We provide 'sustainable solutions' that save marine ecosystems and respond to the climate crisis.

Free marine waste procurement

By establishing a process to receive starfish free of charge from the government, we reduced disposal costs while lowering manufacturing costs.

Marine waste R&D commercialization

Through our proprietary starfish upcycling technology, we achieved a Zero-Waste technical resource circulation.

Sustainable eco-friendly products

We build a sustainable industrial ecosystem with carbon-reducing products that meet strict environmental regulations in global markets such as the U.S. and Canada.
