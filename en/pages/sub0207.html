ECO-SL Product Features

01

Deep-penetrating sealer that prevents road damage

Prevents damage or discoloration to wood, stone, and composite finishes even in severe cold, and suppresses spalling and surface cracking on winter roads, extending the lifespan of facilities.

02

Prevents corrosion of vehicles and facilities

The corrosion inhibitor forms a protective layer on metal surfaces of vehicles and facilities, delivering corrosion protection 300% stronger than conventional de-icers.

03

Contains concrete strength enhancer (VMA)

VMA penetrates deep into concrete, fills voids, and transforms it into a high-strength structure. After de-icing, it dramatically prevents chronic damage to concrete and roads, and shows a remarkable 230% improvement in concrete strength compared to conventional de-icers.
