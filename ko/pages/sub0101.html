About Us

Established on March 15, 2000, Genebiotech Co., Ltd. is headquartered in Gongju-si, Chungcheongnam-do, Republic of Korea. For over two decades, we have harnessed eco-friendly biotechnology rooted in traditional fermentation to develop innovative feed additives, functional foods, and animal pharmaceuticals that benefit livestock, aquaculture, and agricultural sectors.

With our subsidiary DaOne Chemical Co., Ltd. we produce feed additives and the related animal (e.g. Swine, Poultry, Cattle, Fish, Pet, Goat, Horse and Camel) products including but not limited to fermentation-based microbial and soy-peptide ingredients that enhance digestion, nutrient absorption, and stability under harsh gut and processing conditions.

Committed to advancing animal health and productivity, we invest significantly in research and development. At Genebiotech, we connect scientific innovation with tradition to deliver sustainable, high-performance solutions for global animal nutrition.
